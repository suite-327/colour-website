Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Colour collects and processes the following information:

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Photos you choose to capture within the app
  • Color palette preferences and saved matches
  • Device information for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Save your color matches and preferences
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or support

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption. Photos you capture are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your account for backup purposes. We use Supabase for secure cloud storage and authentication.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and data storage
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (can be removed with in-app purchase)
  • Apple Sign In: Optional authentication method

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your saved color matches
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Colour is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
